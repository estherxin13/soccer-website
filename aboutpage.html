About Our Organization

Established in 2000, PB Training Academy has been running under head trainer Peter Bennett, who has lead numerous competitve soccer teams to provincial and national championships. Additionally, his training mentality and dedication not only leads to tremondous success within compettive soccer, he also works 1-on-1 with athletes to develop skill, power and stamina.

Our Mission and Values

At ‘PB Training Academy’, we focus entirely on the needs of the athlete, whether that is at a recreational or competitive level. We strive to develop well-rounded soccer players and help indivuals reach their fitness goals. Athlete-Focussed: The training focusses on the athlete or team's goals and what they want to achieve. The athlete creates the goals with some assisstance from our team. Quality Over Quantity: We strive to make sure that every minute with us is filled with the top-notch training and drills. Inclusion: Our organization does not discriminate based on skill level, years of training, or finanical status. We accept and encourage everyone to participate.
